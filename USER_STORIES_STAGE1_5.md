# User Stories - Stage 1.5

## Story 1: Consistent Secondary Page Headers
As a returning user,  
I want secondary pages to share the same header style,  
So that navigation feels predictable and trustworthy.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. GIVEN I am on a non-home page  
   WHEN the page loads  
   THEN I see a consistent header with brand imprint and back navigation.
2. GIVEN I am on a secondary page  
   WHEN I switch between pages  
   THEN header placement and spacing remain consistent.

---

## Story 2: Modernize Auth & Pricing Visuals
As a new user,  
I want login, registration, and pricing pages to match the main brand style,  
So that the product feels cohesive and high quality.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. GIVEN I open login/register/pricing  
   THEN the visual language matches the homepage style (card, spacing, radius).
2. GIVEN I am in dark mode  
   THEN the pages render with consistent contrast and legibility.

---

## Story 3: Unified Error & Empty States
As a user,  
I want errors and empty states to be consistent across pages,  
So that I always know what to do next.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. GIVEN an error occurs  
   THEN I see a friendly explanation and a clear action (retry or CTA).
2. GIVEN no data exists  
   THEN I see a structured empty state with guidance and CTA.

---

## Story 4: Standard CTA Hierarchy
As a user,  
I want primary actions to be visually clear and consistent,  
So that I can complete tasks faster.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. GIVEN any page with multiple actions  
   THEN the primary button is visually distinct from secondary actions.
2. GIVEN I navigate across pages  
   THEN button styles remain consistent in size, radius, and color.
